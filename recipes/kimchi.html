Kimchi Recipe

Description

Kimchi is a traditional Korean side dish of fermented vegetables and seasonings.

It's made by salting and fermenting vegetables, such as cabbage or radish, and then mixing them with spices. Kimchi is known for its tangy, spicy, and savory flavor.

Ingredients

  • Vegetables
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Salt the vegetables
  2. Marinate the vegetables
  3. Ferment the vegetables
